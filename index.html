About Us

We are a group of highschoolers inpired to help the waning economy due to the COVID-19 pandemic by creating ease of access applications to benefit the user.

What is Foodversity?

Foodversity provides a safe way for everyone to dine out. We give the information about covid hotspots , and provide you with a restaurant in the low covid rate areas. Our mission is to prevent the spread of the virus , while still being able to go out and dine , as it is a big part of many families. With foodversity you will be able to stay safe from the covid-19 hotspots , and be able enjoy the food you love.


Our Mission

Foodversity prometes the food market , while being aware of the situation around us. Covid -19 has created a new normal for all of us. Now that things are opening back up and dinings are starting again we would like to provide a safe way to dine out.
